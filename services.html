What We Offer

Accounting & Bookkeeping

We manage daily records and provide accurate financial statements to keep your business organized and informed.

Tax Services

We handle all aspects of tax compliance, from computation and planning to filing tax returns (including BIR forms) and managing VAT and withholding taxes for both corporations and individuals.

Payroll Management

We streamline your payroll process, generating payslips and ensuring accurate and timely payments, including statutory deductions (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, BIR) and compliance with all relevant tax regulations.

Audit & Assurance

We provide a range of audit services, including internal and external audits, compliance audits, financial statement audits, and special purpose audits.

Business Setup & Compliance

We assist with registrations (SEC, DTI, BIR), obtaining necessary LGU permits (Barangay, Mayor's Permit), and managing business renewals and closures.

Business Consulting

We provide strategic financial guidance, including business planning, financial forecasting, internal controls review, and budgeting and cost management support.

Accounting System Implementation

We help you set up and implement accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero, etc.), develop your chart of accounts, and provide comprehensive staff training.

CFO Services

We offer strategic financial leadership, fundraising support, investor reporting, performance analysis, and KPI dashboard development.

Specialized Services

We handle specialized projects such as due diligence for mergers and acquisitions, feasibility studies, and compliance with government reports (DOLE, PEZA, BOI).
